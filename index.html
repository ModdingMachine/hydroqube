About HydroCubes

HydroCubes is revolutionizing hydration tracking with our smart cube technology. Simply drop the cube in your water bottle and let it do the rest. Our advanced sensors measure your water intake while the cute virtual pet motivates you to stay hydrated.

Smart Features

Bluetooth Connectivity

Seamlessly syncs with your smartphone, providing real-time hydration tracking and personalized notifications to keep you on track with your daily water intake goals.

Digital Pet Widget

Care for your virtual pet by maintaining healthy hydration habits. Watch it grow and thrive as you achieve your daily water intake goals.

LED Display

Quick view of daily water intake with our intuitive LED display. Visual feedback helps you stay on track throughout the day.
